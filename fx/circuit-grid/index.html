About this effect

A procedurally routed circuit board. The algorithm uses a constrained random walk on a grid: each wire picks a starting cell, walks 3–12 steps in cardinal directions, biased to keep going straight 50% of the time, never re-using an edge. The result fills the canvas evenly — no clusters, no empty zones. Bulbs and switches are placed only at real wire endpoints, so they always sit on wires.

Pulses are CSS-style setLineDash with animated lineDashOffset — extremely cheap. Bulbs use radial gradient haloes with sinusoidal pulse phase. Switches auto-flip every 2–8 seconds for life.
