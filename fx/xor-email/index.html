About this tool

How it works: Each character of your email is XOR-ed with a single-byte key (default 42), then hex-encoded. Bots scraping your site see a 32-character hex string with no @; humans clicking the button trigger a scramble + typewriter animation that reveals the real address. Used on every owlos.sk client site.

To use: Edit your email above → hex regenerates automatically → click "Copy HTML" → paste the full snippet anywhere on your site. No build step, no dependencies.
